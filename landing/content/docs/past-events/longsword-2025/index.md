rules_content: |
  ### Participants eligibility

  For the beginners category:
  - No more than 2 years of HEMA experience
  - No medals from any HEMA tournaments
  - No medals from related sports (tap fight, buhurt, olympic fencing)

  For women's category:
  - No experience or restrictions

  ### Competition Rules
  
  This tournament followed [Swordplay 2025 rules](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1b1J8SODsiPEzLVYMIwmDeTJsZSSSjXvkpa2jdEXmz08/edit?tab=t.0) with some changes:
  
  - No single-handed actions allowed
  - More strict requirements for safety and excessive force control
  
  ### Safety Requirements
  
  Equipment regulations follow [Swordplay 2025 gear requirements](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1JCdW4ohQ6L277cryCYQKzPwaejUcQDpKtJgRdBzm9nI/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.x1w5havnmx9z)
